# Financial Market Glossary

## A

**Accrued Interest**: Interest accumulated since the last coupon payment.

**AANA (Aggregate Average Notional Amount)**: Used to determine UMR phase-in thresholds.

**ARM (Approved Reporting Mechanism)**: Entity authorized to report under MiFIR.

## B

**Basis Point (bp)**: 1/100th of 1% (0.01%).

**Bilateral**: Direct agreement between two parties without intermediary.

**Book**: A collection of trades managed together for P&L and risk.

## C

**CCP (Central Counterparty)**: Becomes buyer to every seller and seller to every buyer.

**CDS (Credit Default Swap)**: Derivative providing protection against credit events.

**CFD (Contract for Difference)**: Derivative based on price difference.

**Clean Price**: Bond price excluding accrued interest.

**Close-out Netting**: Netting all trades to single amount upon default.

**Collateral**: Assets pledged to secure obligations.

**Confirmation**: Formal document agreeing trade terms.

**Coupon**: Periodic interest payment on a bond.

**CSA (Credit Support Annex)**: ISDA document governing collateral.

**CVA (Credit Valuation Adjustment)**: Adjustment for counterparty credit risk.

## D

**Day Count Convention**: Method for calculating interest periods (ACT/360, 30/360, etc.).

**Delta**: Option price sensitivity to underlying price.

**Dirty Price**: Bond price including accrued interest.

**DVP (Delivery versus Payment)**: Simultaneous exchange of securities and cash.

## E

**EMIR (European Market Infrastructure Regulation)**: EU derivatives regulation.

**Exposure**: Potential loss if counterparty defaults.

## F

**FIX Protocol**: Financial Information eXchange messaging standard.

**Fixing**: Determination of floating rate for a period.

**FOP (Free of Payment)**: Security transfer without payment.

**Forward**: OTC contract for future delivery at agreed price.

**FRA (Forward Rate Agreement)**: Contract on future interest rate.

**Front Office**: Trading and sales functions.

**Future**: Exchange-traded forward contract.

**FX (Foreign Exchange)**: Currency trading.

## G

**Gamma**: Rate of change of delta.

**Greeks**: Option risk sensitivities (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta, Rho).

## H

**Haircut**: Discount applied to collateral value.

**Hedge**: Position offsetting risk of another position.

## I

**IM (Initial Margin)**: Collateral covering potential future exposure.

**In the Money**: Option profitable to exercise.

**IRS (Interest Rate Swap)**: Exchange of fixed and floating payments.

**ISDA (International Swaps and Derivatives Association)**: Industry body, publisher of standard documentation.

## L

**LEI (Legal Entity Identifier)**: 20-character entity identification code.

**LIBOR**: London Interbank Offered Rate (legacy rate).

**Limit**: Maximum exposure allowed.

**Liquidity**: Ease of trading without moving price.

## M

**Margin**: Collateral required to cover potential losses.

**Margin Call**: Demand for additional collateral.

**Mark-to-Market (MTM)**: Current market value of position.

**Matching**: Comparison of trade details between parties.

**Maturity**: Date contract ends.

**Middle Office**: Risk management and P&L functions.

**MTA (Minimum Transfer Amount)**: Smallest collateral transfer.

## N

**Netting**: Offsetting obligations to reduce amounts.

**Notional**: Principal amount used for calculations (not exchanged).

**NPV (Net Present Value)**: Sum of discounted cash flows.

## O

**OTC (Over The Counter)**: Traded directly between parties, not on exchange.

**Out of the Money**: Option not profitable to exercise.

## P

**P&L (Profit and Loss)**: Financial performance measure.

**PAI (Price Alignment Interest)**: Interest on cash collateral.

**PFE (Potential Future Exposure)**: Possible future exposure.

**Portfolio**: Collection of trades/positions.

**Premium**: Price paid for option.

**PV01**: Price value of 1 basis point.

**PVP (Payment versus Payment)**: Simultaneous exchange of payments.

## R

**Reconciliation**: Matching of records between systems/parties.

**Repo (Repurchase Agreement)**: Sale with agreement to repurchase.

**Reset**: Determination of floating rate for period.

**RFQ (Request for Quote)**: Asking for price quote.

**Risk-Free Rate**: Reference rate without credit risk (SOFR, â‚¬STR).

## S

**SA-CCR (Standardized Approach for Counterparty Credit Risk)**: Exposure calculation method.

**SDR (Swap Data Repository)**: US trade repository.

**SEF (Swap Execution Facility)**: US trading venue for swaps.

**Settlement**: Final exchange of cash/securities.

**SIMM (Standard Initial Margin Model)**: ISDA margin calculation methodology.

**SOFR (Secured Overnight Financing Rate)**: US risk-free rate.

**Spot**: Immediate transaction (T+2 for FX).

**SSI (Standard Settlement Instructions)**: Pre-agreed settlement details.

**Strike**: Option exercise price.

**Swap**: Exchange of cash flows over time.

**SWIFT**: Global financial messaging network.

## T

**T+n**: Settlement n days after trade date.

**Theta**: Option time decay.

**Threshold**: Exposure level before margin required.

**Trade Date**: Day trade is executed.

**Trade Repository (TR)**: Entity storing trade data for regulatory purposes.

**Tri-Party**: Arrangement using third-party agent for collateral.

## U

**UMR (Uncleared Margin Rules)**: Margin requirements for uncleared derivatives.

**Underlying**: Asset from which derivative derives value.

**UTI (Unique Transaction Identifier)**: Trade identification code.

## V

**Value Date**: Date trade takes economic effect.

**Vega**: Option sensitivity to volatility.

**VM (Variation Margin)**: Collateral covering current exposure.

**Volatility**: Measure of price variation.

## Y

**Yield**: Return on investment.

**Yield Curve**: Graph of yields across maturities.
